The Challenge We Face

Cebu's waste management crisis requires immediate action. Here's how we're making a difference.

Current Problems

📈

Daily Waste Problem

Cebu generates over 500 tons of waste daily, overwhelming our systems.

⚠️

Illegal Dumping

Unregulated dumping pollutes our waters and endangers public health.

🗑️

Limited Landfill Capacity

Our landfills are reaching capacity, creating an urgent crisis.

Our Solutions

✔

Barangay Recycling Centers

Establishing local centers to process recyclables and reduce landfill burden.

✔

Community Cleanup Drives

Regular cleanup events to remove waste and prevent environmental damage.

✔

Waste Education Seminars

Teaching proper waste management and sustainable practices.

Community action + education = long-term impact for Cebu.
